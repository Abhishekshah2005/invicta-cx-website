# HP-14 — Ambient loop · rack-focus screen → face

**Phase A · Homepage · Supporting Ambient · Video · VID-LOOP · NOT STARTED**

## Purpose

A cinematic rack-focus from a glowing screen to a specialist's focused face — the
pivot from "the system" to "the person." Emotion: the human behind the interface.
Conversion: dramatizes the whole thesis (human > machine) in one subtle move.

## Nano Banana Prompt (poster / mid frame)

> Editorial two-plane composition: a softly glowing monitor in the foreground
> (abstract UI, unreadable) and a support specialist's focused face in the
> mid-ground, warm window key + cool-warm screen fill, shallow depth of field.
> Muted charcoal knit. Real, composed micro-expression. Shot on ARRI Alexa, 85mm
> prime, f/1.8; warm-editorial grade (ink shadows, ivory highlights, muted
> midtones, one crimson accent); fine film grain, soft roll-off; photorealistic
> 4K, ultra-detailed skin texture.

**Negative Prompt**

> stock photo, posing, fake smile, RGB neon, teal-orange, HDR, plastic skin,
> readable screen text, logos, watermark, text, 3d render, extra fingers.

## Flow Prompt

> 8–10s seamless muted loop. **Camera:** locked. **Focus pull:** begins sharp on
> the foreground screen, then a slow, motivated **rack-focus to the face** (~3s),
> holds on the person, then eases focus back toward the screen to match the first
> frame. **Subject motion:** the specialist blinks, breathes, a faint shift of the
> eyes. **Timing:** slow and weighted. **Lighting:** monitor glow breathes subtly.
> **End frame:** focus + expression matched to frame one for a clean loop. 24fps,
> grade unchanged.
