# HP-15 — Ambient loop · night ops floor

**Phase A · Homepage · Supporting Ambient · Video · VID-LOOP · NOT STARTED**

## Purpose

A wide ambient loop of the operation breathing at night — 24/7 made visible.
Emotion: always-on reliability, calm scale. Conversion: reinforces "every
timezone," a quiet trust signal behind a chapter.

## Nano Banana Prompt (poster / first frame)

> A wide cinematic night shot of a warm modern operations floor at low light —
> layered pods of specialists lit by monitor glow, a soft city or window glow
> beyond, one restrained crimson practical deep in the frame. Warm dark shadows,
> layered depth, not a cubicle grid. Muted wardrobe. Shot on ARRI Alexa, 35mm
> prime, f/2.2; nocturnal warm-editorial grade (deep ink shadows, ivory speculars,
> one crimson accent); documentary candor, cinematic chiaroscuro, fine film grain,
> soft roll-off; photorealistic 4K.

**Negative Prompt**

> stock photo, posing, RGB neon, rainbow, teal-orange, HDR, cubicle grid, call
> center rows, plastic skin, readable screens, logos, watermark, text, 3d render.

## Flow Prompt

> 12–14s seamless muted loop. **Camera:** an extremely slow, motivated push-in or
> lateral drift (~3%). **Subject motion:** the floor "breathes" — a couple of
> distant specialists shift or turn once; monitor glows flicker softly.
> **Timing:** very slow, weighted, unhurried. **Lighting:** subtle ambient
> breathing, steady crimson practical. **End frame:** matched to first for a clean
> loop. **Transition:** cross-dissolve the seam. 24fps, grade unchanged.
