# HP-06 — Homepage Operations (the establishing frame of the operation)

**Phase A · Homepage · Operations · Image · HERO-STILL · Status: NOT STARTED (on deck)**

## Purpose

The homepage argues: hard moment → the person who steps in → **the operation
behind them** → proof. HP-06 is that third beat made visible — the proof that
Invicta is _"not a call center, but an operating system for human trust."_ Emotion:
**quiet awe + reassurance** — a real, warm, world-class operation with soul.
Conversion: it converts a claim into belief; visitors who _see_ a credible,
humane operation trust Invicta with their own customers and scroll on to the CTA.

## Nano Banana Prompt

> A wide cinematic establishing photograph of a warm, modern customer-experience
> operations floor at golden hour. Sharp foreground (right third): a support
> specialist in her 30s at a matte-oak workstation, three-quarter from slightly
> behind, mid-work — leaning in, listening, the cool-warm glow of her monitors on
> her focused composed face. Behind her the room recedes in layered depth: soft
> pods of two–three specialists at natural desks (someone turning to a colleague,
> someone reading) — never a grid of cubicles, never a call-centre. Low golden sun
> rakes through large rear windows, blooming warm; a single small crimson practical
> glows deep in the background bokeh. Warm materials — oak, brushed metal, warm
> concrete, a few plants, paper, a cooling coffee. Screens carry only abstract
> unreadable soft UI light. Wardrobe elevated-casual, muted/desaturated — charcoal,
> ink, stone, oat — one figure a whisper of crimson. Calm, expert, quietly alive.
> Generous negative space and glow on the left of frame. Shot on ARRI Alexa, 35mm
> cinema prime, f/2.8, natural golden-hour window light + monitor glow with warm
> falloff; warm-editorial grade (ink shadows, ivory highlights, muted midtones, one
> crimson accent); documentary candor, unposed real specialists, natural
> micro-expressions; luxury-magazine composition; fine film grain, soft highlight
> roll-off; photorealistic 4K, ultra-detailed skin and material texture.

**Consistency:** same visual language / grade / wardrobe / lighting as the
Homepage Hero + Human Conversation — this is the _wide_ of that world.
