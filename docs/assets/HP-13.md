# HP-13 — Ambient loop · hands over keyboard

**Phase A · Homepage · Supporting Ambient · Video · VID-LOOP · NOT STARTED**

## Purpose

A quiet ambient loop of the work itself — hands moving with intent over a
keyboard. Emotion: focused craft, "the work is happening right now." Conversion:
adds subliminal life/motion to a chapter without stealing focus; premium film
texture.

## Nano Banana Prompt (poster / first frame)

> Editorial macro-ish close-up of a specialist's hands moving over a mechanical
> keyboard on a warm oak desk, a coffee and paper nearby, cool-warm monitor glow
> and a soft window key, shallow depth of field, the room a warm blur behind.
> Muted charcoal sleeve, one crimson detail out of focus. Real skin and material
> texture. Shot on ARRI Alexa, 50mm prime, f/2; warm-editorial grade (ink shadows,
> ivory highlights, muted midtones, one crimson accent); fine film grain, soft
> roll-off; photorealistic 4K.

**Negative Prompt**

> stock photo, staged, RGB keyboard LEDs, gaming keyboard, teal-orange, HDR,
> plastic skin, readable screen text, logos, watermark, text, 3d render, extra
> fingers, distorted hands.

## Flow Prompt

> 10-second seamless muted loop. **Camera:** locked/near-locked, an almost
> imperceptible slow drift-in (~2%). **Subject motion:** hands typing in a calm,
> natural rhythm — not frantic — one pause to reach for the mouse and back.
> **Timing:** unhurried, weighted. **Lighting:** monitor glow flickers softly with
> the imagined UI; window light steady. **Micro-interactions:** a finger adjusts,
> the coffee steam drifts. **End frame:** hands return to the home row, matching
> the first frame for a clean loop. **Transition:** cross-dissolve the seam if
> needed. 24fps, grade unchanged.
